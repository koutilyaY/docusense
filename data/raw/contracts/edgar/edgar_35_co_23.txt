WHEREAS , Issuer has enacted share repurchase programs, as amended most recently pursuant to the Cooperation Agreement, dated September 6, 2024 (the &#8220; Program &#8221;), for the repurchase of a maximum of up to $10,000,000 of its common stock, par value $0.0001 per share (the &#8220; Common Stock &#8221;)&#59; WHEREAS , pursuant to the Program, 21,983 shares of Common Stock have been repurchased under at a cost of $57,057.45 as of the close of business on September 30, 2024&#59; WHEREAS , as part of the Program for the repurchase of its Common Stock, Issuer desires to implement the instructions set forth herein (the &#8220; Instructions &#8221;)&#59; and WHEREAS , Issuer desires to appoint Broker as its single broker to repurchase shares of Common Stock on its behalf in accordance with these Instructions and the Program&#59; NOW, THEREFORE , the Issuer and Broker hereby agree as follows&#58; 1. Trading Requirements . (a) Broker shall effect a repurchase (each a &#8220; Purchase &#8221;) of shares of Common Stock on each day on which The Nasdaq Capital Market (&#8220; Nasdaq &#8221;) is open for trading at a price not in excess of the price per share limitation set forth in Schedule A to these Instructions. (b) Broker shall purchase shares of Common Stock on the open Nasdaq market or in block purchases, subject to the (i) price per share limitation set forth in Schedule A to these Instructions, (ii) the termination provisions for these Instructions as set forth in Section 2 below, and (iii) any other limitation as set forth in these Instructions. 2. Effective Date&#47;Termination . The Instructions shall become effective as of November 1, 2024, and shall terminate upon the earlier of&#58; (a) May 15, 2025&#59; (b) such time as the aggregate cost of all shares of Common Stock purchased under these Instructions equals $9,942,942.55&#59; (c) the receipt of written notice from the Issuer requesting the termination of the Instructions&#59; provided that Issuer may request termination under this Subsection 2(c) whenever a failure to do so would cause or contribute to, or allow or contribute to the continuation of, a breach of a covenant or obligation of Issuer in connection with any obligation other than those arising solely under these Instructions&#59; or (d) any time any trade contemplated hereunder shall result in a violation of, or adverse consequences under, applicable securities laws. 3.
